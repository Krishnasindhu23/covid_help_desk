Do's

Track your levels

On a regular interval, keep checking your SPO2,pulse with the help of pulse oximeter.If the SPO2 level is above 95,it is normal reading.If it is below 90, immediate medical assistance is must!

Do's

Handwash & Sanitize

Do practice good hand hygiene and wash your hands frequently, especially before you eat, after you use the restroom, blow your nose or cough or before you touch your face. Good hand hygiene includes washing your hands for more than 20 seconds, including in between your fingers and under your nails and using plenty of soap and warm water. If you can’t wash your hands, use an alcohol-based sanitizer with more than 60% alcohol.

Do's

Stay Informed

Do stay informed of the situation as events and advisories are rapidly evolving. Credible resources include the Centers for Disease Control and the World Health Organization.

Dont's

Sleeping posture

If you are suffering with COVID, it is adivisable not to lie on your back while you sleep.Sleep only on the stomach or on the side.

Dont's

Mental Health

DONOT leave them with their thoughts in their room alone. COVID takes a toll on mental health as well.They can have their phone or laptop, and you can talk to them often, and make sure they don't feel unwanted.

Dont's

Avoid Socialising

Don’t share utensils or drinking glasses with others, including family members.Don't use common public places like elevators or stair-cases or parking slots to the maximum extent.
