Citrus Fruits

Citrus Fruits like lemons,limes,grapefruit and several types of oranges which are rich in vitamin-C encourage our immune system to produce more white blood cells(WBC),which are responsible for improving our immunity.

Kiwi

Kiwi is a fruit which can be used as medicine for people with asthma and high blood pressure.It also helps curing constipation and other intestinal problems.

Tulsi & Ginger

Tulsi leaves and Ginger are the Natural Anti-virus foods.Boiling 3-4 tulsi leaves and making it Tulsi drink (or) making Amla(ginger) juice part of our diet will boost our immunity levels.

Turmeric Milk

The golden spice-Turmeric serves as an Anti-oxidant and Anti-inflammatory agent.Boiling turmeric with milk and ginger aids in healing wounds and curing throat infections.
